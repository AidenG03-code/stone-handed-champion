Alex Pereira – Stone Handed Champion

This story follows Alex Pereira, a man who grew up in poverty in Brazil and overcame alcoholism to become one of the most feared fighters in the world. From working at a tire shop to winning championship belts in kickboxing and the UFC, Alex’s journey is about redemption, discipline, and honoring his indigenous roots.

The story highlights his legendary rivalry with Israel Adesanya, his emotionless fight style, and how he carries his tribe's pride every time he steps into the cage. He fought Adesanya twice on the regional scene and beat him twice including a knockout win and then followed him to UFC to TKO him again for the UFC middleweight championship
